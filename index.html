Text Sentiment Analyzer

This tool will allow you to conduct a sentiment analysis on written text. The system analyzes the text and computes a sentiment score which reflects the overall sentiment, tone, or emotional feeling of your input text.

To perform your sentiment analysis, simply type or paste some text into the box below and click the "Analyze Text!" button.
